# AI for Customer Success

This repository contains lightweight notes, examples, and experiments exploring how AI and automation can improve Customer Success outcomes in SaaS and platform businesses.

This work reflects hands-on exploration from a Customer Success leadership perspective, focused on improving adoption, renewal outcomes, and Net Revenue Retention (NRR) rather than building production code.

Focus areas:
- AI-assisted onboarding and adoption
- Renewal forecasting and Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Customer health signals and success planning
- Executive QBRs and ROI storytelling
- Scalable CS playbooks and workflows
